# travel-plans

**My Future Travel Plans - Singapore**

This repository contains my ideas for travel in the near future. 

I plan to travel to Singapore to explore the Garden City, experience its incredible blend of cultures, try world-famous hawker food, and see its futuristic architecture!

Here are some websites that I will use:

- [Visit Singapore Official Guide](https://www.visitsingapore.com/)
- [Lonely Planet - Singapore](https://www.lonelyplanet.com/singapore)

## Must-See Locations

- **Marina Bay Sands** - SkyPark Observation Deck, the Helix Bridge, and the Spectra light show
- **Gardens by the Bay** - Supertree Grove (vertical gardens), Cloud Forest, and Flower Dome
- **Jewel Changi Airport** - The HSBC Rain Vortex (the world's tallest indoor waterfall) and Canopy Park
- **Sentosa Island** - Universal Studios Singapore, S.E.A. Aquarium, and Siloso Beach
- **Chinatown** - Buddha Tooth Relic Temple, street markets, and historic shophouses
- **Little India** - Sri Veeramakaliamman Temple, Mustafa Centre (24-hour shopping), and vibrant flower stalls
- **Kampong Glam & Haji Lane** - Sultan Mosque, trendy boutiques, and incredible colorful street art
- **Orchard Road** - A massive 2.2km shopping boulevard lined with iconic retail malls

## Things to Eat

- **Hainanese chicken rice** - Try Tian Tian Chicken Rice at Maxwell Food Centre
- **Chilli crab or black pepper crab** - Famous at Jumbo Seafood or Newton Food Centre
- **Laksa** - Spicy coconut noodle soup, especially Katong style
- **Kaya Toast & soft-boiled eggs** - Served with traditional Kopi (local coffee) at Ya Kun Kaya Toast
- **Satay skewers** - Best experienced at Lau Pa Sat's outdoor Satay Street at night
- **Char Kway Teow and Hokkien Mee** - Classic, smoky stir-fried hawker noodles
- **Roti Prata** - Flaky flatbread served with savory fish or mutton curry
- **Rainbow bread ice cream** - Traditional ice cream blocks wrapped in sweet bread from an Orchard Road street vendor

## Things to Experience

- Visit the **Night Safari**, the world's first nocturnal wildlife park
- Watch the **Garden Rhapsody** light and music show beneath the glowing Supertrees at night
- Dine at a bustling local **Hawker Centre** for an authentic, Michelin-approved street food experience
- Take a flight on the **Singapore Flyer** for panoramic views of the city skyline
- Stroll down **Haji Lane** to explore local indie fashion labels and massive wall murals
- Walk through the indoor **Cloud Forest** conservatory to see a mountain covered in exotic plants
- Experience the distinct multicultural neighborhoods all within a short MRT (subway) ride of each other

## Where to Buy Pokémon Cards

- **Pokémon Center Singapore** - The only official permanent flagship store outside of Japan, located inside Jewel Changi Airport. Features exclusive Singapore-themed pilot and cabin crew Pikachu merchandise and official card sets.
- **Don Don Donki** - The Singapore branch of Japan's Don Quijote (locations at Orchard Central, Clarke Quay, and more) which frequently stocks Japanese Pokémon card booster packs and themed snacks.
- **Sanctuary Gaming** - Located at Orchard Gateway, a huge local hub for card players selling booster boxes, sleeves, and rare singles.
- **Plaza Singapura Hobby Shops** - A shopping mall known for housing pop-culture and anime retail chains like *Hakken!* that stock official gaming merchandise.
- **Toys "R" Us (VivoCity Flagship)** - Features a massive dedicated toy and trading card section with official booster releases.
